Key Features

Collaborative Feedback

Grn Lite allows authors to receive real-time feedback from beta readers, helping them refine their manuscripts and improve their work.

Author-Reader Interaction

Authors and readers can communicate directly, allowing for clear expectations and better collaboration throughout the review process.

Project Budgeting

Authors can set their project budgets and find beta readers that fit their needs and budget, creating a fair and efficient process for both sides.

Beta Reader Profiles

Beta readers have detailed profiles that showcase their previous feedback, pay scales, and preferences, helping authors select the best fit for their project.

Editor Collaboration

Editors can work alongside authors and beta readers, providing additional insights and professional editing services to ensure the manuscript reaches its full potential.

Seamless Payments

Grn Lite offers integrated payment systems that allow authors to pay beta readers and editors securely, making transactions easier for both parties.

For Authors

Submit your work and get valuable feedback from dedicated readers. Choose from various review services including proof-reading, first draft review, and final draft review.

For Readers

Join our community of passionate readers. Choose your preferred review tasks and help authors perfect their work while earning recognition for your contributions.

Our Mission

Creating a collaborative space where authors and readers come together to create exceptional literary works.
